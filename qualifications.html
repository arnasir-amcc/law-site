During my pupillage applications, I joined a local Bar Crown Court providing Clerk services to the Courts. I was solely responsible for remanding defendants to custody at the discretion of the Court.

Owing to the nature of the court environment, I have accumulated a vast compendium of well-networked figures in the law circles within London, and have had many opportunities to shadow experienced judges as part of my commitments towards my pupillage aspirations.

During my tenure at the Crown Courts, I have been honoured to have been selected for several internal promotions, further displaying my dedication towards legal contexts of all levels.

I completed my LLB at Middlesex University London, achieving a 2:1 with honours.

Middlesex University was the first official commitment I made towards making my mark in law, and the initial stepping stone that led to my Call to the Bar at Lincoln's inn. On campus, my lecturers and peers were pivotal in supporting and encouraging my aspirations as a barrister. As part of the alumni network at Middlesex, I have been invited to deliver a speech on my pathway to the Bar, to further the motivations of interested students.
